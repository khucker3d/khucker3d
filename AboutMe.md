## 🎮 Tech Art
I am a senior technical artist with deep experience building and modernizing real-time art, animation, and FX pipelines for Unity-based games and land-based slot content. I specialize in translating artistic vision into performant, scalable systems by combining shader development, tooling, and workflow design to support both artists and engineers. My expertise includes real-time animation and FX systems, custom HLSL shader development (sprite animation, flipbooks, recoloring, glow effects, UV scrolling), and reusable component-driven workflows that reduce reliance on baked animation and image-sequence pipelines.
