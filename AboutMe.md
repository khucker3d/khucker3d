## 🎮 Tech Art
I’m a Senior Technical Artist and leader specializing in building scalable, production-ready pipelines that bridge art and engineering in real-time environments. My work focuses on enabling teams to create high-quality content efficiently through tools, shaders, automation, and clear technical workflows. I’ve spent my career owning end-to-end content integration, from DCC export pipelines to in-engine implementation, optimization, and validation. I enjoy solving complex production challenges, reducing friction for artists, and designing systems that are both performant and easy to use. A key part of my workflow includes leveraging AI as a tool, not a replacement for foundational knowledge. I use AI to assist with debugging, exploration, and accelerating iteration, but I always validate outputs, understand underlying systems, and explore alternative solutions. I believe strongly in learning the fundamentals first, applying AI responsibly, and contributing back to the learning process as both the user and the technology evolve.
