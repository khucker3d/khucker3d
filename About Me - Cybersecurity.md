## About Me - My Cybersecurity Journey

---

Hi, my name is Kellie Hucker. I am a U.S. Army Military Police veteran and a Senior Technical Artist in the mobile game industry, now transitioning into cybersecurity. My background combines structured security discipline with years of experience building technical systems, pipelines, and tools in production environments.

My cybersecurity interests center around network security and network engineering, with a growing focus on blue team defense, detection engineering, and GRC. I am particularly interested in understanding how data moves through systems, how threats are identified through logs and telemetry, and how secure network environments are designed, monitored, and maintained.

My goal is to build practical, well-documented security systems that are both effective and scalable. This includes:
- Blue team lab environments
- SIEM pipelines and log analysis workflows (Wazuh, Splunk)
- Detection engineering
- Endpoint monitoring, security, and hardening
- Structured and repeatable lab environments

I have successfully built a home lab for blue team training, where I design and validate detection workflows using real-world scenarios.

I have also developed a foundational understanding of hardware security risks, including how to mitigate hardware-based infections and reduce exposure through secure handling practices, trusted installation methods, and system hygiene fundamentals.

In parallel, I am progressing through the Google Cybersecurity Professional Certificate, and have completed Courses 1 through 5 as part of my foundational training.
